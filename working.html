CAP FOLDING MACHINE

The anti-theft ring is a small, but extremely important part situated beneath the surface of the bottle cap. It is designed to breaks off on first use, leaving the ring on the bottle to indicate any tampering. This folding process is done by, interestingly, the Folding Machine which folds the extended part of the anti-theft ring inward.

CAP COOLER MACHINE

The plastic caps that come directly from the molding process are hot and can bend or be easily damaged if not cooled. This is the reason why the Cap Cooling Machine is capable of cooling the caps immediately and uniformly allowing every cap to maintain its perfect shape. When this machine is incorporated into the assembly line, producers can realize increased reliability and productivity decreasing on the amount of time that is spent addressing production disruptions.

CAP SLITTING MACHINE

The tamper-evident band is crucial feature for maintaining the assurance of product quality and customers’ safety.This band plays a security function that eliminates the chance of interference, apart from creating trust among the customers on the credibility of the product. The Slitting Machine is built with a pneumatic system with compressed air support and computer control from PLC; therefore, the cutting is very accurate to the expected depth of the cap.

CAP LINING MACHINE

To provide a closer fit and retain the CO2 concentrations in beverages with high content of carbonation, TPE liners are integrated with the cap shell. With full automation and PLC control, the system produces a smooth and efficient result based on the defines requirement and specification added on these liners helps the cap to performs well giving a secure seal that protects it during storage and transportation.
