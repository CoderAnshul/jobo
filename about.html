About Jobo India

Jobo India, a proud partner of Jobo Machinery, has the vision to transform the cap manufacturing business. Having started its journey in 1987 with absolutely the creation of the plastic bottle cap injection mound first, Jobo Machinery has been in cap compression molding business for more than thirty years. Today we are present in 51 countries worldwide and are well known for quality, innovation, and industry-specific knowledge in the field of plastic cap production.

Starting from the injection molding that was in the early stages of production to the evolution of the super advanced 4th generation Cap Compression Molding Machines, Jobo Machinery has consistently set industry benchmarks. The caps manufactured by our machines are specially developed to cater the needs of the ever-growing sectors like beverages, cosmetics and many more industries with high quality and accuracy.

What distinguishes us is far than our technology or tools. Jobo India makes it possible to offer support in every step of the journey to completion. If what you need is machine setup, technical support, spare parts, or increasing production capacity, let us help your business grow. Partner with Jobo India and allow us to take care of the rest so you can focus on what matters – bringing out the best caps to the global market
